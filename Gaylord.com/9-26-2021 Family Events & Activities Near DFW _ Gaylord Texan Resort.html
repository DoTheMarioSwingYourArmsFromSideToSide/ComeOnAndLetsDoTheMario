YOUR DREAM RESORT & WATER PARK EXPERIENCE AWAITS

Located on the shores of the majestic Lake Grapevine, Gaylord Texan Resort offers family-friendly entertainment and the thrilling, ten-acre Paradise Springs Water Park complete with slides, a zip line, and 600-foot-long lazy river. Experience the wonder of our 4.5-acre soaring glass atrium featuring lush gardens and beautiful architecture, and indulge with our extraordinary amenities including spacious rooms, luxurious spa, delicious food and drink, and shopping. Experience a Summerfest of more excitement, activity, and entertainment at Gaylord Texan Resort.
