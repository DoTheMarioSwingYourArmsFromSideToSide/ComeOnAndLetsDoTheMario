YOUR DREAM RESORT & WATER PARK EXPERIENCE AWAITS

Located on the shores of the majestic Lake Grapevine, Gaylord Texan Resort offers family-friendly entertainment all year round. Experience the wonder of our 4.5-acre soaring glass atrium featuring lush gardens and beautiful architecture and indulge with our extraordinary amenities including spacious rooms, luxurious spa, delicious food and drink, and shopping. Experience more excitement, activity, and entertainment at Gaylord Texan Resort.

EXPLORE TEXAS

The unique town of Grapevine, Texas, just outside Dallas, provides plenty to do. Located only minutes from DFW airport and near many local attractions like LEGOLAND® Discovery Center, Cowboys Golf Club, Historic Downtown Grapevine, as well as an abundance of water activities on the lake, there's no shortage of places to explore.
