Who We Are

Description

Our updated 1940s casita in the heart of downtown Shiner, Texas is a perfect getaway. Previously Kuby's man cave, this home will make you steps away from Spoetzl Brewery, local Welhausen Park, Shinerville Coffee Shop, B'Marie's Boutique and a variety of restaurants. Also, a short drive to the beautiful and historic Saint Cyril and Methodious Catholic Church. Grab a drink at the favorite local hangouts, Howard's and Shiner’s On Sixth. We hope you will enjoy your visit to the "cleanest little city in Texas!"

The Casita

Kuby's Place has 1 queen bedroom with 1 full bathroom ensuite. The kitchen is fully equipped with kitchen island seating. Bring your own food to cook out on the grill and enjoy the wonderful, large patio. The bathroom should have everything you need: towels, soaps, essentials and hair dryers. Pets up to 50 lbs. are also allowed for a $25 fee.

Guest Information

GUEST ACCESS:

Guests have access to the entire rental. Parking for 2 cars is behind the casita. Guests will walk around to the front of the rental to access from the patio.


INTERACTION WITH GUESTS:

We will be onsite for most stays, please reach out via phone or text should you need anything during your stay. A list of local area restaurants and places to check out during your stay will be provided.
