We offer quiet country sites that are close to town.
 

 

Centrally located in Shiner, the Cleanest Little City in Texas. We are just over an hour away from Austin, San Antonio, Houston and Victoria. We have 29 pull through sites (25 x 70) for RV's with 15/30/50 amp power service, septic and water. All utilities are included in our rates.


Shiner RV Park is located at 554 County Road 350, the road just north and adjacent to the beautiful Shiner Green Dickson City Park that has walking trails, tennis courts, children's play area and picnic areas. The RV Park is a quiet country setting less than a mile from the conveniences of the City of Shiner.

  Area Attractions  
 

Area restaurants and hot spots to visit:

  • Fiddler's Frolic Fiddle Contest & BBQ Cookoff
  • Shiner Restaurant and Bar
  • Werners
  • Tenth Inning
  • Antique Art and Beer
  • Home Sweet Shiner
  • Shiner Beer Run
  • The Great Austin to Shiner Pedal

Interesting Places:

  • Spoetzl Brewery, home of Shiner Beer, founded in 1909 making it the oldest Texas independent brewery. Tours are available Monday thru Friday. Make sure you visit the "Hospitality Room" for a taste!
  • Green-Dickson Municipal Park, a beautiful park featuring walking trails, children's playground, covered picnic areas with barbecue, lighted tennis courts.
  • The Gaslight Theatre , the center of social life from 1895, Shiner's first Opera House hosts amateur productions held three times a year.
  • Welhausen Park , one square block featuring a historic bandstand and children's playground.
  • Local Museums - Cigar Factory and Green Cabin Museum and Wolters Memorial Museum showcasing area history and antiques.
  • Painted Church Trail, Shiner is home to one of the most beautiful churches on the trail, St Cyril and Methodius Catholic Church. Dedicated in 1921, the church's amazing Romanesque Revival style features a beautiful altar, painted murals, and stained glass imported from Bavaria. A truly inspiration place to visit.

These are some of the local attractions, it is just a short drive to many more of the area's rich history and conveniences.

  Park Information  
 
  1. AUTO & RV's: Drive at 5 MPH. Please do not park on grass. Mechanic work (including oil changes) is not allowed. Washing vehicles or RV's is not allowed.
  2. PETS: All pets must be on a leash at all times. Please clean up after your pets. Noisy or aggressive pets will be removed.
  3. SEWER: All grey and black water is to be dumped into sewer. Do not put grease, oil, hazardous material, bleach, coffee grounds, paper towels, sanitary napkins, or tampons in sewer.
  4. ALCOHOL AND DRUGS: The use of drugs, drunkenness, disorderly conduct will not be tolerated and are grounds for eviction.
  5. QUIET TIMES & CURFEW: Quiet time is 10:00 PM to 8:00 AM. No loud talking, radios or noise during these hours.
  6. CAMPFIRES: Campfires are not allowed in the park.
  7. TRASH/LITTERING: Trash is to be placed in the dumpster. Please if you smoke, do not throw cigarette butts on the ground.
  8. SITES: Sites are to be left clean. Your cooperation is greatly appreciated.
  9. FIREWORKS / FIREARMS: No fireworks or firearms are allowed in the park.
  10. CHECKOUT: Checkout time is 12:00 NOON
Shiner Boggy Creek RV Park will not be held liable to any guest or their visitors for injury or damages, however caused, to any property or persons on the premises.
Be advised, Shiner Boggy Creek RV Park is in a flood zone. It is the guest responsibility to monitor weather in event of severe conditions that may cause flooding and take precautions by removing RV and property from the park.
