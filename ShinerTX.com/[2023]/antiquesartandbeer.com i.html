Beer!!

Antiques, Art & Beer has over 250 Beers (not to mention 150 Wines) to choose from … have you tried them all??

Homemade Fudge

We make our own fudge in house.  Every variety you can think of including some made with our very own Shiner beer.  Come see what we’re making this week!

Paddywax: Soy Wax Candles

Triple-Milled Shea Butter & Olive Oil Soaps, & Hand Washes – all Glass Containers are re-purposed & recycled Wine Bottles.

Buy A Shiner Pint Glass … Add The Beer For Only A BUCK!!!

Buy a Shiner Pint Glass for around $7.50 and for only a buck MORE fill it with the Shiner BEER of your choice!  

Pet Friendly

Antiques, Art & Beer is pet friendly … we have a big enclosed patio outback with access to fresh, running water so please bring your pet’s private water bowl with you.  Now, if you forget, we might be able to scrounge one up for you.

We’ve got Cows!

Antiques, Art & Beer is now in the Dairy Cow Business!  These ladies showed up last week and they’re looking for love!!!  Come choose yours today!

Armadillos anyone?

These guys MUST find homes SOON!!!  They’re digging up my shop looking for grubs…yuck!  PLEASE adopt one today?!?!?! They are ADORABLE!  We have the daddies, the mommies and the babies!  

Zebras everywhere!

Anybody seen a circus?  I think I took a wrong turn… oh, well – I’m at Antiques, Art & Beer and I’d LOVE to go home with YOU!  I promise I’m house-trained!!!  

A Wild Soap Bar

Pure and mild natural soaps that are handmade in the old world tradition right here in Texas with certified organic vegetable oils, wild and organic herbs, and only pure essential oils. Why? Because organic soap matters. So go ahead..take a wash on the wild side!

Handmade Domino Tables

We have a limited number of these beautiful, unfinished, handmade domino tables. They have cup holders, and they are gorgeous! They are handmade by a gentleman who lives about an hour out of Shiner.  First come first serve! $400.00 each.

Game Room

Have you played in our back room? We have Dominoes, Jenga, Playing Cards, Monopoly, Life, Sorry, Scrabble, Word Search, Texas Hold ‘Em, and more!  

I Drank A Shiner in Shiner, Texas!!!™

Antiques, Art & Beer is the only place your Shiner can be certified!  Get your “I drank a Shiner in Shiner, Texas” certificate today!
