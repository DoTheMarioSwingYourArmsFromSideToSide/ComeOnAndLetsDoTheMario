About Dr. Rivas

Dr. Rivas is a fully committed pediatric specialist that has been in practice for 30 years. He completed his internship and pediatric residency at Rutgers University Hospital -Children's Hospital of New Jersey in 1992. He practiced initially in Austin, TX where he resided for 13 years before moving to Gonzales with his family in 2006. He has extensive experience in hospital and ambulatory care. as well as in taking care of complicated and healthy newborns, premature infants, children and adolescents up to the age of 21. Beyond taking care of the whole spectrum of pediatrics he has special interests in Dermatology, Asthma/Allergies, Infectious Diseases and critically ill newborns and infants.

How We Help

Preventative Care

It is important for all children ages 0-18 to receive an annual physical to ensure they are growing and developing properly.  We make sure that your child's well-being is safeguarded by checking their vision, hearing and physical development as he or she grows from childhood into adolescence. We provide preventive physical exams, immunizations, and address onset illnesses and injuries, as well as on-going primary care needs. 

 

Acute Symptoms

We offer diagnostics for many acute symptoms such as ear infections, strep throat, flu, colds, and many other illnesses.
