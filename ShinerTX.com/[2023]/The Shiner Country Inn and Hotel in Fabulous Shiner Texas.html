Welcome to the Shiner Country Inn

A Boutique Hotel in lovely Shiner, Texas

The Shiner Country Inn is the best place to stay to experience all things related to Shiner, Texas and beyond. Our 10 eclectic rooms and stunning amenities are designed to bring you the ultimate Texas retreat. Relax at Welhausen Park across the street, take a stroll through town, and unwind in your beautifully designed country suite.

Although our Shiner Hotel captures the essence of the region, we urge you to go out and explore. Our hotels location is ideal for traveling to many of the major cities youll want to check off your Hill Country bucket list. Road trip to Luling or Hallettsville in just 20-minutes, Austin, San Antonio and Houston are all 90-minutes away. Our country inn is also conveniently located just 5-minutes from the historic Shiner shops, where you can find even more things to do to during your vacation.
