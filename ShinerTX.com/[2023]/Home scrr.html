No matter if it's your first time at an Escape Room or you are a professional escape artist, you will have an INCREDIBLE ADVENTURE in any one of our five different themed rooms.


Escape Rooms are the perfect entertainment venue for friends, family and company or corporate team building experiences. It is the ultimate bonding experience for friends, families, or co-workers and more than that, it is AWESOME FUN!


Immersive and challenging, your team of four or more players will need to work together during your mission. You will search for hidden objects, decipher codes and analyze puzzles. Communication is essential as you explore your environment and seek solutions. The key to success is Teamwork.

Sorry, no walk-ins. Games must be booked online at least 3 hours in advance and all players must arrive at least 20 minutes before start time for pre-game briefing.
