Welcome to Kloesel's Steakhouse and Bar

Harvey and Diana would like to welcome you to Kloesel's Steakhouse & Bar, the perfect place to enjoy a great meal in a historic setting.

We serve only choice premium cuts of aged beef, which Harvey personally selects, cuts trims and cooks to perfection. We also serve a variety of pasta, seafood, chicken, tex-mex dishes, salads, and sandwiches. When you visit, don't forget to check the board specials of the day. A lunch plate special is served daily. We also have pies, a variety of cheesecakes and other delicious desserts. 

Kloesel's Bar is also a great time. Come and drink, visit, and dance to your favorite tunes. Check out our new sound system and dance floor lighting.

 

Kloesel's Steakhouse and Bar has been listed in the Houston Chronicle's "Best places to eat on a Texas road trip"
