Just For Kids Learning Center provides a loving, learning environment where the intellectual, social, and developmental potential of each child can be realized in a positive, stimulating, and creative manner that is consistent with effective teaching practices.


A safe and attractive learning environment is provided where children can feel comfortable playing, resting, eating, and learning. Children spend time outdoors when weather permits as well as indoors.


Structured learning activities that are developmentally appropriate, stimulating, and fun for each child are an integral part of each day. Basic concepts (shapes, colors, numbers) are introduced, and children learn through using manipulative and constructive materials, creative dramatics, music, art, movement, and scientific experiences on an individual and small group basis.
