The Internet Age

 In the Internet age, it's important for almost every business to have a presence on the World Wide Web. Many small businesses are finding that a Web Page can be an important way for new customers to find them, and to communicate effectively with existing customers. Recent reports estimate the growth of the Internet (in number of users) at 35% per year! Our experience tells us that is probably an understatement.

Although it's possible to use "do-it-yourself" methods to get on the Internet, most new users do not know the tricks for effective page design, use of compact graphics, and posting files to an Internet Web Server. Instead of "trial-and-error", we can offer you our experience to guarantee quality results at reasonable prices -- done well, done on time!

Our Experience

 Since 1997, we've designed Internet Web Pages for several small and large companies. As a result of an Internet presence, many of our clients have received new orders from customers who might never have found them otherwise.

  • Kaspar Companies Home Page : This large manufacturer of sheetmetal and wire products had no presence on the Internet until we created their pages from scratch. Mostly text, with effective use of photographs and small graphics. This was our first client and in many ways is some of our best work.

  • Boedeker Plastics' Home Page : Our second client, this job started as a "make-over" of existing pages, but developed into a complete overhaul -- now our head designer works his "day job" at this company.
