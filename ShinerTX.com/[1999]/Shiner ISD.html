School Calendar



Elementary
   Principal: Jonetta Crain  594-3251
           Includes grades Pre-K - 6
            The elementary is a four star school in the state of Texas.  Time is spent trying to help each child
            reach their highest levels.  Teachers spend many hours preparing themselves for daily instruction
            so that all students may excel.  This means that many different learning styles are being used.
            Dedication and hard work are the characteristics of the staff.
 

Jr. High
    Principal: Trey Lawrence    594-3131

            Includes grades 7-8
            During these two years students are being prepared to enter the high school curriculum areas with a
            solid foundation in the basic subject areas.  Students during this time are also exposed to sports on
            the competitive level.  Fundamentals are taught so that as they enter high school, they will be able to
            compete academically as well as atheletically.
 

High School
    Principal: Trey Lawrence    594-3131

            Includes grades 9 - 12
           Academics are stressed to help individual students reach their highest potentials.  Atheletics play an
            important part in the school curriculum with empahsis on developing teamwork and sportsmanship.
