The Kaspar Companies

The Kaspar Companies include an integrated group of design and manufacturing operations for high quality products in sheetmetal, wire, and plastics. Our production capabilities include wire, sheetmetal, and tubing cutting, forming, stamping, electroplating, spray painting, powder-coating, and high-quality silkscreening . Our staff can provide professional design, production and testing for your custom manufactured product. We can build your product in any quantity, from one-of-a-kind prototypes to high-volume production runs.

The Kaspar Companies' business operations include:

  • Kaspar Wire Works manufactures stock and custom products from wire, sheetmetal, and tubing including baskets, fan grilles, display stands, easels, food processing equipment, computer frames and stands, furniture components, and saddle rigging. Also featuring a new line of material handling equipment such as carts, storage bins, baskets, and trucks. If it's made from wire, sheetmetal or tubing, give us a call!

  • Kaspar SHO-RACK provides coin-operated and free-vend newspaper racks, mechanical and electronic coin mechanisms, periodical display racks, in-store fixturing and store counter displays to the publishing industry. SHO-RACK Service Centers also offer used newsracks and honorvends, factory closeouts, and complete refurbishing and reconditioning services for racks and coin mechanisms. Let us help you show off your newspaper, free publication, or brochures!

  • BASSICK by Kaspar manufactures casters, wheels, glides, floor locks, positioning jacks, hinge clamps, and related products for industrial, commercial, and institutional applications. If your product needs to move, call us!

  • Kaspar Electroplating performs metal surface preparation, electropolishing and electroplating in a variety of materials including zinc chromate, nickel, and chrome using barrel and/or rack plating methods. Give us a chance to put a high-quality finish on your product!

  • Advances by Kaspar Electroplating in wastewater treatment are now available for commercial and industrial applications. KEC's new electrocoagulation unit (KASELCO) can reduce your heavy-metal and other suspended solids to environmentally acceptable levels with fewer added chemicals than ever before possible. Contact us for more information!

  • Kaspar Die & Tool offers design and machining services for forming, stamping, progressive, coil-feed and other high-volume production tooling for metal, plastic, leather, and cloth manufacturers. We can help you produce your product faster!
