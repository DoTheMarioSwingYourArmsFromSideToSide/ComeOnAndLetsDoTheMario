Maps & Directions to Shiner

Back to SHINER Home Page

Shiner, Texas is located about half-way between Houston and San Antonio at the intersections of Highways 95 and Alternate 90 (also called "90-A") in South-Central Texas. If you'd like to visit us, check out the maps and directions found on this page.

Directions to Shiner from Houston - 120 miles:

  • Leaving Houston, take IH-10 WEST towards San Antonio.

  • IH-10 WEST passes through or nearby the following towns on the way to Flatonia:

    10 miles to KATY, 22 more miles to SEALY, 20 more miles to COLUMBUS, 26 more miles to SCHULENBURG, 25 more miles to FLATONIA

  • Exit IH-10 at FLATONIA (Exit 661). Turn LEFT under freeway, pass Dairy Queen, to stoplight on Highway 90.

  • Turn RIGHT on Highway 90 WEST and drive through town.

  • Turn LEFT on Highway 95 SOUTH (before crossing under the bridge!) You will cross RR tracks immediately.

  • Follow Highway 95 SOUTH through Moulton (8 miles) to Shiner (10 more miles.)

Directions to Shiner from San Antonio - 90 miles:

  • Leaving San Antonio, take IH-10 EAST, following the signs "To Houston".

  • Follow IH-10 EAST for about 60 miles. You will pass many exits, including several for Seguin. Continue on IH-10 EAST past Seguin for about 22 more miles.

  • Exit RIGHT onto Highway 183 SOUTH to Gonzales.

  • Follow Highway 183 SOUTH for about 17 miles to Gonzales.

  • Turn LEFT on Alternate Highway 90 (also called Highway 90-A) EAST.

  • Follow Highway 90-A EAST to Shiner (about 18 miles.)

Directions to Shiner from Austin - 80 miles:

  • As you leave Austin, take Highway 183 SOUTH.

  • Continue to follow Highway 183 SOUTH for about 25 miles to Lockhart.

  • Continue on Highway 183 SOUTH for about more 15 miles to Luling.

  • Continue to follow Highway 183 SOUTH by turning LEFT at the stoplight immediately after you cross the railroad tracks.

  • In about 3 miles, you will pass under IH-10. Continue to follow Highway 183 SOUTH for about 17 more miles to Gonzales.

  • Turn LEFT on Alternate Highway 90 (also called Highway 90-A) EAST.

  • Follow Highway 90-A EAST to Shiner (about 18 miles.)

Directions to Shiner from Victoria - 40 miles:

  • As you leave Victoria, take Highway 77 NORTH.

  • Continue to follow Highway 77 NORTH for about 20 miles to the intersection of Highway 111 (at the stop sign.)

  • Turn LEFT on Highway 111 (WEST).

  • Continue on Highway 111 WEST -- drive into and through Yoakum, to the intersection of Highway 77-A (a Dairy Queen is on the corner.)

  • Turn RIGHT on Highway 77-A (NORTH).

  • Follow Highway 77-A to the intersection with Highway 95 (about 3 miles.)

  • Turn LEFT on Highway 95 SOUTH towards Shiner.

  • Follow Highway 95 SOUTH to Shiner (about 8 miles.)
