The Shiner Country Inn is just the place to get away from the hustle and bustle of the big city ... only 80 miles from Austin and just over 100 miles from San Antonio or Houston ... we're 20 miles south of IH-10. Stop in for a night or a weekend!

The Shiner Country Inn is conveniently located just 3 blocks from downtown Shiner on Highway 90A, across the street from historic Welhausen Park.


Owned and operated by Macy and Nina McCorkle, all of our rooms have been newly decorated with a country charm that is pleasing and comfortable.

We take special care to see that our guests feel right at home. After a busy day of seeing our historic downtown, the sights and shopping, relax on our front porch swing and watch the rest of the town go by!

Ask us about our Special Occasion Packages (for Birthday, Anniversary, Honeymoon, Valentine's Day, or other special event.) Let us decorate your room and make your stay extra memorable!
