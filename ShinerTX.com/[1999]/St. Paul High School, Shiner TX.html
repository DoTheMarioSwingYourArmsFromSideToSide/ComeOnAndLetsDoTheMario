We are a small parochial school located in rural South Central Texas. Our enrollment is approximately 260 students in grades Pre-Kindergarten - Grade 12. We offer a complete curriculum including religious education, mathematics, language arts, fine arts, and the sciences, as well as an entire array of athletics.
