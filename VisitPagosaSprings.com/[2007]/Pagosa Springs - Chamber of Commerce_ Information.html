It's fun to shop in Pagosa!
From knick-knacks and keepsakes to fine art, you’ll find a wide variety of eclectic shops lining the streets of Pagosa Springs. Log furniture and western wear shops are nestled along side art galleries
and outdoor gear. No matter where you shop, you’ll find a friendly face and great merchandise in our
old-fashioned little southwestern town.
