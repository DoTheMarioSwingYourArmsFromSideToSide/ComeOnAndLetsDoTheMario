Come to Pagosa ...
Pagosa Springs, located in the southwest corner of Colorado, is nestled in the "sunbelt" and boasts more sun-drenched days than the state of Florida. Pagosa is a gateway to numerous attractions within the Four Corners area.

Just where is Pagosa Springs Colorado?

At an elevation of 7,079 feet and situated at the junction of Highways 160 and 84, Pagosa Springs is within easy driving distance of several major cities:

Durango, CO · 61 miles
Cortez, CO · 101 miles

Farmington, NM · 98 miles
