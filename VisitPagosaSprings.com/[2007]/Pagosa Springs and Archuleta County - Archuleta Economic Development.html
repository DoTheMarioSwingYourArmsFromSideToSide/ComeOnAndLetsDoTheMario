The Archuleta Economic Development Association is a 501 c(6) non-profit organization whose sole purpose is to enable, grow and sustain economic development in Archuleta County. Our mission is to provide business development and support in Archuleta County in order to nurture a diverse, local year-round economy that supports the needs, values and quality of life of our community. We do that by offering business attraction/incentives, creation, relocation & expansion services, mentoring & education, and financial assistance. AEDA succeeds when our businesses succeed. When our businesses succeed, our community thrives, and the quality of life, values, and needs of the community are met.

As part of our overall mission, this web site contains a variety of important resources for the business owner, site selector, or entrepreneur looking for a great place to do business:

  • A section on Why Archuleta County for information on demographics, climate, housing, lifestyle, & community services.
  • A detailed business relocation and expansion section for site selectors and businesses covering incentives, development, infrastructure, taxes, services, workforce, and enterprise zone information.
  • Useful links and resources of the local government, business and community organizations.
  • Small business mentoring including personal mentoring as well as business plan training/ marketing courses.
  • Retail and Real Estate information to assist new and existing business owners to find the right space to place your business.
