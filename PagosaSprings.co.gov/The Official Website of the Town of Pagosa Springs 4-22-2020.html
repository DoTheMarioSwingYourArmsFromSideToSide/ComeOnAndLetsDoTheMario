Home Page

Welcome to the Town of Pagosa Springs' official website. This website has been created to provide you with convenient access to local information and services. 

Town Hall is located at 551 Hot Springs Blvd. in downtown Pagosa Springs. We are open Monday through Friday from 8:00 am to 5:00 pm.

  • Mailing Address is PO Box 1859, Pagosa Springs, CO 81147
  • Phone is 970-264-4151
  • Fax is 970-264-4634
COVID-19 Information

Colorado is on a statewide Stay-at-Home Order as designated by Governor Polis in Executive Order D2020 017 

You should stay home as much as possible except for critical activities including:

  • Obtaining food and other household necessities including medicine
  • Going to and from work if you are a critical employee (see list of critical businesses)
  • Seeking medical care
  • Care for dependents or pets
  • Caring for vulnerable person in another location
  • Participating in outdoor recreation at a legally-mandated safe distance of six feet or more from other parties

 For more details including the full executive order, FAQ, press release and list of critical businesses go to the Colorado State Web Portal.

Local COVID-19 Assistance Programs

The Town of Pagosa Springs is partnering with local and regional organizations to provide assistance programs for businesses, non-profits, and individuals impacted by COVID-19 closures. For more information go to our COVID-19 Information Page.
