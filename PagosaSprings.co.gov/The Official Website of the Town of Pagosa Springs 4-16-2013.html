Home Page

Welcome to the Town of Pagosa Springs' official website.  This website has been created to provide you with convenient access to local information and services.  If you are unable to find the information you are seeking please feel free to call the Town Hall or stop by and see us.

Town Hall is located at 551 Hot Springs Blvd in downtown Pagosa Springs.  We are open Monday through Friday from 8:00 am to 5:00 pm.

  • Mailing Address is PO Box 1859, Pagosa Springs, CO 81147
  • Phone is 970.264.4151
  • Fax is 970.264.4634
