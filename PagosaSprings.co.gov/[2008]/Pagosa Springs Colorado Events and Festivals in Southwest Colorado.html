Pagosa Springs Colorado Events and Festivals in Southwest Colorado

Step back 50 years and remember hometown festivals, unique outdoor events, feet-stomping music, and hopping rodeos year round in Pagosa Springs…Or if you want a little more upscale flair, our renown classical musicians, professional drama productions, and talented array of artists might fit your style a bit better. Here is just a small taste of our major events and festivals or for a full schedule of events (View our Event Calendar):
  • Winterfest
  • Pagosa Fiber Festival (View Website)
  • Pagosa Folk and Bluegrass Festival (View Website)
  • Fourth of July Parade & Fireworks/Carnival/Park to Park Art Show
  • Red Ryder Rodeo, Busted Spur, and Little Britches Rodeo Series
  • Music in the Mountains (View Website)
  • Archuleta County Fair/Demolition Derby (View Website)
  • Folk Corners Folk Festival (View Website)
  • ColorFest/Mountain Chile Cha Cha/ Wine Festival
  • Christmas in Pagosa
