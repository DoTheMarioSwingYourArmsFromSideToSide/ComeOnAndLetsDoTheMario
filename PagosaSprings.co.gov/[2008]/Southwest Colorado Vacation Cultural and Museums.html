Southwest Colorado Vacation Cultural and Museums

From ancient civilizations to our Western heritage, Pagosa Springs is full of history. When you are ready to stimulate your mind, we have our fair share of cultural activites, including:

·         Chimney Rock Archeological Area features ancient Pueblo Indian ruins, guided educational tours, full moon programs, and Native American festivals. (View website)

·         Fred Harman Art Museum is home of Red Ryder and Little Beaver Comic Strips, Fred Harman paintings, and other western memorabilia (View website)

·         Parelli Natural Horsemanship World Headquarters & Museum – Learn how Parelli has transformed the horse training industry through innovative conferences, successful training techniques, and natural horse equipment. In 2008, they will open a brand new world headquarters and museum in Pagosa Springs (View website)

·         Rocky Mountain Wildlife Park – Get up close and personal with Colorado wildlife, including mountain lions, bears, elk, deer, porcupine, wolves, and more.

·         San Juan Historical Museum – Learn how the pioneers lived in the Pagosa Springs area from the 1890s to the early 1900s. (View website)
