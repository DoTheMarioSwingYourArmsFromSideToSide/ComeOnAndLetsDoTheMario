Bayfield

Welcome to our Town web site!  If you are a Bayfield resident, we hope this site will provide you with the information you are looking for and perhaps also something new.  With this web site, we would like to offer you some of our services in a more efficient way.  You can find important phone numbers and some forms on line.  We will add more over time to improve the convenience of your local government.  As always, we welcome suggestions, simply fill out the website suggestion form and submit it to us.

This website provides helpful information about the Town, our community, and the surrounding areas.  For more information, please contact us and let us know how we can help you.
