02/23/08 - 20th And Final Pagosa Springs Arts Council Photography Contest at Moonlight Books

20th And Final Pagosa Springs Arts Council Photography Contest at Moonlight Books.

Attend the Opening Reception February 23 from 3 - 6 pm.

Photos on display: Feb. 9th - March 8th, 2008

This year is different from past contests -- This year YOU are the Judge.

1.  Open to all amateur and professional photographers.

2.  Entries must not have been previously exhibited in Archuleta County.

3.  Each exhibitor may submit a total of TWO entries in either black & white or color, but only two entries in any one category.  Each photo must be at least 5x7 (unmatted/unmounted) and the total circumference of the matted/mounted/framed photo may not exceed 85".

4.  All photos must be ready to hang: matted/mounted or framed.  a)  Framed photos must have screweyes and wire hanger only.  b)  Matted and/or mounted photos need plastic stick-on hangers that are available from local framers and at Moonlight Books.

5.  There will be a $2.00 entry fee per photograph.

6.  Entries will be accepted at Moonlight Books until 5pm, Wednesday, February 6.

7.  Entries will be on display at Moonlight Books from Sat., Feb. 9th through Sat., March 8th.  All photos must remain on display for the duration of the exhibit.

8.  If photo is for sale, a 25% commission will be deducted at the time of the sale by PSAC.  Please take this into consideration when pricing your photo. 

9.  Categories:  1-Domestic Animals   2-Architecture   3-Autumn Scenic   4-General Landscape   5-Patterns/Textures   6-Sports   7-Flora   8-People   9-Up Close   10-Winter Scenic   11-Black & White   12-Wild Animals   13-Sunrise / Sunset   14-Open  Does not fit in any other category   15-Special Techniques (color or black & white)  Any type of manipulation, including computer, belongs in this category.

10.  Entries must be picked-up Saturday, March 8th or Monday, March 10th, between 9am and 5pm, not before.  After Wednesday, March 12th, any remaining photos will be donated to the Arts Council for fundraising purposes.

11.  The Pagosa Springs Arts Council and Moonlight Books will not be responsible for loss or damage of any photographs entered in the contest.  Although, care will be taken with your entries.

Judging: During the first two weeks on the exhibit, EVERYONE will have the opportunity to judge the entries.  We will provide an easy judging form.  On February 23rd - 1st, 2nd, and 3rd place ribbons will be awarded in each category based on the number of votes received.
