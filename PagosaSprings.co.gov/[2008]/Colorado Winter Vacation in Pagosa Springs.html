Colorado Winter Vacation in Pagosa Springs

We've got the snow...plenty of it...in fact, nearby Wolf Creek Ski Area is known for having "the Most Snow in Colorado". At an average of 465 inches on the mountain, we don't have to worry about making snow. Mother nature provides...in abundance.

When planning your next Colorado ski vacation, you might not think of Pagosa Springs. But once you discover our laid-back town, amazing powder, non-existent lift lines, world famous hot springs, and incredible scenery, you will join the ranks of those who come back every year. Beyond our legendary slopes, you might consider some of our other winter activities:
