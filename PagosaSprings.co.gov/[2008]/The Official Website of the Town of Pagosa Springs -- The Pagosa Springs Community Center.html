The Pagosa Springs Community Center
The Pagosa Springs Community Center

Welcome to our Community Center!

Our 20,800 square foot Community Center is open to every day of the week for your public or private event.

The Community Center has the following amenities to serve the community:

  • A large multipurpose room – for private parties, banquets, music venues, presentations, and indoor recreation. 
  • A catering kitchen adjacent to the multipurpose room used for special events. Restrooms that include showers, lockers and baby changing stations.
  • A computer center with DSL that enables people to utilize technology and the internet for free! 
  • 2 conference rooms including on wired for full range media options, for small group meetings, trainings, and parties. 
  • An art center – complete with a dry media space, wet media space, and a darkroom. 
  • An office and reception area, with a lounge.
  • The Silver Foxes Den including an office, lounge, and a dining room/conference room. 

If you would like more information about the Community Center, please call Mercy Korsgren at 264-4152 x22
