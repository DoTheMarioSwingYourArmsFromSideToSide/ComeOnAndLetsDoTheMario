"Night Blooming Cirrus"

Many of the Choir members performed in the Community United Methodist Church's Concert held on April 11.  Checkout the Gallery for some familiar faces.

The PSCC Mother's Day Concerts will be at the High School auditorium on May 10th(Sat) at 7:00 pm and on the 11th(Sun) at 4:00 pm.

The music selection Includes:
  • Joyful, Joyful Day
  • Omnia Sol
  • Glory, Glory, Hallelujah
  • Instrument of Peace
  • Alleluia Fanfare
  • New Born Again
  • I Hear America Singing
  • America the Beautiful
  • Steady My Trembulin' Soul
  • Change the World
  • Anything Goes
  • Dream a Dream
  • Walking In Memphis
  • What a Wonderful World
The Jazz Group will sing Yesterday and Something Latin.

      All concerts are free - our gift to the Community
