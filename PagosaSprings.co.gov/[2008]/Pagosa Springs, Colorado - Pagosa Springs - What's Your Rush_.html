Pagosa Springs - What's Your Rush?

 Is it the exhilaration of watching the sunrise atop one of our amazing San Juan mountain peaks…or the stimulating sensation of hooking your first rainbow trout on the San Juan River running through Pagosa Springs? Could it be the chilling plunge through whitewater rapids…or hiking to one of our thundering waterfalls? With three million acres of wilderness to play in, you’ll find every outdoor adventure you could hope for – trails abound for every level, from high alpine meadows to lakes to jagged ridges with heart-pounding plummets. Come winter, it’s a different pair of shoes, but just as inviting. Best of all, you can experience the adventure of nature in a setting that still remembers the value of a smile…

What’s Your Rush?... when our small town atmosphere and endless forest allows you to sense the true meaning of vacation...Refresh your spirit in a place away from the crowds that encompasses the beauty of living off the beaten path…Restore your sense of fun while skiing through the most snow in Colorado…Reconnect with family and friends over a cup of coffee while listening to the river rushing by…or maybe rediscover yourself in our warm, healing hot springs that help you slow down long enough to take in all that you’ve been missing. Come to Pagosa Springs and enjoy simplicity on a grand scale.
