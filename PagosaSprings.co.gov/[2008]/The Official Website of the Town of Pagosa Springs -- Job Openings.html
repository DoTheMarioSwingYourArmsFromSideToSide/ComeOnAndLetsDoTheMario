Job Openings

The Town of Pagosa Springs is accepting applications for the following positions:
Completed applications must be submitted to Town of Pagosa Springs, P.O. Box 1859, Pagosa Springs, CO  81147-1859 by 5 p.m. March 3, 2008. The Town is an EOE.

Heavy Equipment Operator/Maintenance Worker. Operates medium and heavy construction and maintenance equipment and large trucks, and performs skilled and semi-skilled work in the maintenance, repair, construction and operation of streets, geothermal and sewer utilities, drainage structures, and assists in parks. Maintains, services and makes repairs to equipment operated. Education and experience required: Any combination of education and experience equivalent to a high school diploma (GED); and three years of related experience in equipment operation. Possession of a valid Class B CDL required. Salary range $30,528-$42,222.

Parks Maintenance Worker I. Maintains the Town parks and other assigned grounds and facilities including recreation facilities, ball fields and related installations.Operates mowers, pickup trucks, small tractors and hand tools, and all-terrain vehicles. Education and experience required: Any combination of training and experience equivalent to graduation from high school, or general education degree (GED); and some related experience. Salary range $28,749-$37,290. Complete job descriptions are available at Town Hall. Applications are available at Town Hall, 551 Hot Springs Blvd., online at www.townofpagosasprings.com.

 

The Town of Pagosa Springs does not accept unsolicited resumés or applications.

The Town is an Equal Opportunity Employer.

The following employment application forms may be downloaded and filled out to be e-mailed or faxed.  

To e-mail your application, please download the word version of the application and save it to your desktop (you must have microsoft word to do this).  Next, fill out the application completely and save the application again before closing it.  Finally, open your e-mail program and address your e-mail to: topsclerk@centurytel.net, with the position desired in the subject line.  Be sure to attach the application that is saved on your desktop!

To fax, mail or hand deliver your application, download the pdf version and fill out the application completely.  Fax the application to:  (970) 264-4634. 

Resumes and cover letters are also accepted with an application.
