KWUF 1400 AM is country music and local talk radio at its very best: all the new country hits with a liberal sprinkling of classics, plus local topics on "Good Morning Pagosa" every weekday. KWUF 1400 AM is also home to local high school sports broadcasts and nostalgia.

KWUF 106.3 FM is a great mix of adult contemporary, classic rock, oldies, and the wildly popular "Blues, Jazz, etc."


We feature personalities who know their music and the tastes of both local and traveling listeners.


We're LIVE & LOCAL for much of each weekday, with local news, weather, sports, outdoor recreation information and longtime local favorites like Good Morning Pagosa and Tradio.


We keep you up to date with CNN News at the top of each hour, 'round the clock, and with local weather each hour.


We're truly a local station, involved with local Community Events and Projects, serving the important and fast growing Archuleta County and surrounding areas.
