Change a Light Program

The Town of Pagosa Springs and LPEA has partnered with ENERGY STAR, a program of the U.S. Environmental Protection Agency and the U.S. Department of Energy in the "Change a Light Pledge" program. In this program, we are asking LPEA members to consider pledging to change one incandescent bulb in your homes to an ENERGY STAR qualified compact fluorescent bulb (CFL).

The ENERGY STAR Change a Light campaign is a national effort to focus public awareness on the economic and environmental difference that the small step of installing a compact fluorescent light bulb can make, when multiplied by hundreds of thousands of consumers.

See how much you can save with the Lightbulb Calculator.

CFL resources:

  • Information on clean up and proper disposal of CFLs from ENERGY STAR and the Environmental Protection Agency
  • Tri-State CFL mercury information sheet

Please go here "Change a Light Pledge" to make your pledge.

Please consider sending this link to your friends and family, asking them to take the pledge as well (simply copy the address above into the body of your e-mail).

We have set a goal of 100 pledges but we hope to greatly exceed this amount. Check out how many pledges we have received.
