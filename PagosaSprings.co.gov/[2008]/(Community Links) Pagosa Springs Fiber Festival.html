... a celebration of ...

the efforts of a growing cottage industry
involved in fiber-related businesses -
spinning, weaving, knitting,
felting, designing patterns.

the efforts to preserve
a rural lifestyle and landscape
by providing the small livestock raisers
a venue to promote their product.
