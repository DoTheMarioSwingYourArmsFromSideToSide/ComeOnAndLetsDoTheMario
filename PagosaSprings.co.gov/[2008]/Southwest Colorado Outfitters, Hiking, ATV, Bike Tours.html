Southwest Colorado Outfitters, Hiking, ATV, Bike Tours

Southwest Colorado is known for its big game hunting including trophy elk hunting, mule deer and turkey hunting. Pagosa Springs is also becoming well-known for having the largest contiguous wilderness area in the State of Colorado. With more than 3 million acres of National Forest and more wildlife than people, the chances of spotting an elk or deer are very high. To experience the best of our natural surroundings, you could hike, bike, 4-wheel, pack, or climb!
