Service First

The San Juan National Forest (Forest Service) and the San Juan Field Office (Bureau of Land Management) are pioneering a concept known as Service First. Service First is a partnership strategy to provide better customer service and be more cost effective in the delivery of that service to users of the public land in southwest Colorado.

The San Juan Public Lands Center is the only organization in the country providing leadership in all aspects of land management for the two agencies as one unit. Shared FS/BLM offices in Pagosa Springs, Durango, and Dolores oversee the three San Juan National Forest Ranger Districts, BLM Field Offices, and Canyons of the Ancients National Monument.

Visit the Service First Web site. and/or read a summary of the San Juan Public Lands Service First venture.

 San Juan Public Lands Offices' Hours


 San Juan Public Lands offices are open the following hours:

DURANGO
The San Juan Public Lands Center, 15 Burnett Court, Durango, is open 8 a.m.– 4:30 p.m., Monday–Friday, closed for all federal holidays. For more information, call (970) 247-4874.

BAYFIELD
The Columbine Ranger District and Field Office, 367 Pearl St., Bayfield, is open 8 a.m.–4:30 p.m., Monday– Friday, closed for all federal holidays. For more information, call (970) 884-2512.

SILVERTON
The Silverton Public Lands Center, 1246 Blair St., Silverton, is closed for the winter.

DOLORES
The Dolores Ranger District and Field Office, 29211 Highway 184, Dolores, is open 8 a.m.–4:30 p.m., Monday–Friday, closed for all federal holidays. For more information, call (970) 882-7296.

The Anasazi Heritage Center and visitor center for Canyons of the Ancients National Monument, 27501 Highway 184, Dolores, has office hours of 8 a.m.–5 p.m. Monday–Friday and Museum hours are 9 a.m.–5 p.m. (March through October), 7 days a week. From November through February business hours are 8 a.m. - 4:30 p.m. and Museum hours are 10 a.m. - 4 p.m., 7 days a week. Closed for all federal holidays. For more information call (970) 882-5600 or visit www.co.blm.gov/ahc or www.co.blm.gov/canm.

PAGOSA SPRINGS
The Pagosa Ranger District and Field Office, 180 Pagosa Street, Pagosa Springs, is open 8 a.m.–4:30 p.m., Monday–Friday, closed for all federal holidays. For more information, call (970) 264-2268.

 Most Forest Service and BLM roads are not snowplowed, so access is limited by road conditions.

For more information, call (970) 247-4874
