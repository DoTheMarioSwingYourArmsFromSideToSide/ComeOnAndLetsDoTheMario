Leave your mark on the Community Center!
We have a couple of programs that you can leave your mark on the Community Center. With a personalized laser engraved brick(s) or be part of our Donor Tree.   It's the perfect gift to honor a friend or a loved one...or to show your support.  The bricks will be prominently placed at entry pathways to the Center for everyone to see.  Add a leaf to the Donor Tree in our Lobby by having your name or that someone special name engraved in a leaf of your choice.  It's the perfect gift to honor a friend or loved one...or to show your support for the Pagosa Springs Community Center.  If you have not contributed please do so now.  Remember, this is your center and this is your community.

Brick Donation - Make your gift today of $100 for each 4" x 8" brick or $200 for an 8"x8"

Donor Tree - Buy a leaf.  Bronze Leaf - $100 - $499, Gold Leaf - $500 - $4999, Platinum (Silver) $5,000 - $49,999, Platinum Stone $50,000                                                                         

Cristin Robertson will take your order.  Call 264-4152 ext. 22 or e-mail Cristin at cristinr@centurytel.net
