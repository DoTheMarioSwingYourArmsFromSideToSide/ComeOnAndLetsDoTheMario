The Ross Aragon Community Center

Welcome to our Community Center!

 

Our 20,800 square foot Community Center is open to the public.

The Community Center is located next to the Town Hall at 451 Hot Springs Blvd.  
For more information on the community center please call 970.264.4152
Or email us at: lgutierrez@pagosasprings.co.gov

Our Hours are Monday - Friday 8:00am to 5:00pm.  (Saturday & Sundays by special request)  

 

Please visit our Upcoming Events Page to see current open registration classes and for current events offered here at the Ross Aragon Community Center! 

The Community Center has the following amenities to serve the community:

  • A large multipurpose room â€“ for private parties, banquets, music venues, presentations, and indoor recreation. 
     
  • A warming catering kitchen adjacent to the multipurpose room for special events.
     
  • Restrooms that include showers, lockers and baby changing stations.
     
  • A computer center with WiFi that enables people to utilize technology and the Internet. 
     
  • Conference rooms for groups of all sizes. 
     
  • An office and reception area with a lounge.
