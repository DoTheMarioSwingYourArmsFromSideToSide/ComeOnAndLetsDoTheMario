Things To Consider Before Purchasing A Humidifier

Proper Humidification Can:

• Reduce vulnerability to infection by keeping mucous membranes moist and allowing cilia (tiny hairs in the nose and throat) to continue working to eject foreign matter caught in the air.
• Prevent dry, itchy skin.
• Relieve cold symptoms.
• Relieve chapped lips.
• Reduce unpleasant static electricity.
• Help keep valuable furnishings from cracking.

While everyone agrees on the benefits of proper humidification, finding the right humidifier can be a real chore. Too many humidifiers have too many problems. Problems like difficult and expensive maintenance, water tanks that are hard to fill and carry, designs that unintentionally create ideal breeding grounds for mold and bacteria—and then there’s that awful white dust that settles on everything!
