Browse Fans By Blade Size
If you know the size of the room in which you wish to install your ceiling fan, browsing by blade size is a great way to make your selection. Huntermakes fans ranging in size from 36” to 60” measured from blade tip to blade tip. For maximum performance and efficiency, Hunter recommends the following fan size to room size matches:

Blade Size    Room Size    Square Footage
36” 10 x 10 100 sq. ft.
42” 12 x 12 144 sq. ft.
44” or 48" 15 x 15 225 sq. ft.
52” or 54” 20 x 20 400 sq. ft.
56” 22 x 22 485 sq. ft.
60” 25 x 25 625 sq. ft.
