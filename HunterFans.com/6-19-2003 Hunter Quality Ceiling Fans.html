Hunter Quality
John and James Hunter built their first ceiling fan in 1886. Times may change, but Hunter's uncompromising concern for quality has always remained constant. So it comes as no surprise that Hunter fans purchased more than eighty years ago are still running today.

From our patented Original® and AirMax® motors, to our perfectly matched and balanced real wood blades, to our rich, lustrous finishes, Hunter fans are made to be the very best. Details like factory-sealed precision bearings, heavy-gauge copper windings, and heavy-duty capacitors are just a few of the details that mean quality at every turn.

Take a close look at a Hunter, and you’ll see the quality in the fit, finish and materials. Listen to a Hunter fan, and you’ll hear the quality in its whisper-quiet operation. Relax under a Hunter fan, and you’ll feel the quality in the power and consistency of the air movement. Purchase a Hunter fan, and rest assured that you’re bringing home the finest fan available, backed by a lifetime warranty. That’s the Hunter quality promise.
