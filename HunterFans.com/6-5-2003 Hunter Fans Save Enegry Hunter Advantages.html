Hunter Fans Save Energy
In addition to adding beauty and comfort to your home, Hunter ceiling fans are a great way to save energy and help lower your monthly utility bills!

Summer Cooling
In the summer, by setting your Hunter fan to run counterclockwise and create a downdraft, your fan creates a “wind chill” effect that makes you feel up to 8° cooler. That means you can significantly reduce your use of air conditioning, and save up to 40% on summer cooling costs!

Winter Heat Reclamation
In winter, set your Hunter fan to run clockwise and create an updraft. This recirculates hot air trapped at ceiling level without creating the cooling “wind chill” effect. You get more even, comfortable heating while reducing your winter heating bills up to 10%!

Note: Your savings may vary based on climate, building construction and thermostat settings. Savings shown are on average with fan at low speed setting.

ENERGY STAR® Labeled Hunter Ceiling Fans
ENERGY STAR is the U.S. Government Environmental Protection Agency (EPA) program. ENERGY STAR is devoted to helping Americans save energy and to helping companies create products that save energy.

• ENERGY STAR labeled Hunter ceiling fans move air about 15%–20% more efficiently than a typical fan.

• ENERGY STAR labeled Hunter fans can save homeowners up to $30 a year.

• ENERGY STAR labeled Hunter ceiling fan/light combination models are about 40% more efficient than a typical fan, using 60%–70% less electricity for lighting than other models.

• Characteristics of an energy-efficient Hunter ceiling fan include:
    - Efficient, powerful, whisper-quiet Hunter motors to maximize air movement.
- Some models feature light kits with compact fluorescent bulbs. These bulbs provide soft, warm illumination and last longer than incandescent lights.
