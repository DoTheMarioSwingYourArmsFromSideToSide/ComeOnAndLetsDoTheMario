Hunter Ceiling Fans
It should come as no surprise that our world-famous ceiling fans are Hunter’s best-known products. After all, we’ve been making them for nearly 120 years. And today, we make them better than ever!

Consider this your ceiling fan central. From here, you can learn how to select a ceiling fan, how to properly size a fan to a room and all about the many Hunter accessories you can add to your fan. You can also browse for your new fans by finish, browse by blade size, and browse by product category, including medium and small room fans, large room fans, low profile fans, outdoor fans, and our showroom-only Hunter Prestige® fans.

This site showcases three types of Hunter fans: our core fan products, our Hunter Prestige® line of showroom models and our Hunter Exclusive fans.

The Hunter core product line
features our most popular fan styles, finishes and features.

The Hunter Prestige® product line
offers “the best of the best”—premium fans found only in designer showrooms.

Hunter Exclusive fans
are unique designs that can only be found at specific retailers.
