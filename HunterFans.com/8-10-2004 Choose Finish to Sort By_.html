Browse Fans By Finish
A deep, rich finish is “the finishing touch” that adds beauty and durability to every Hunter fan. All Hunter finishes are tarnish, scratch and rust resistant. Our totally unique Hunter Bright Brass Finish® and other plated metallic finishes are the result of our multi-stage grinding and buffing process. Hunter painted finishes are created with thick, powder-coated paint to provide extra richness. And our hand-painted finishes include a second layer of artisan-applied paint to make each fan one of a kind. To browse for fans by finish, click on the appropriate listing below.
