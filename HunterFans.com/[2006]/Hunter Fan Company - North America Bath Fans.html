Proven Performance
All Hunter Bathroom Fans are tested and certified by the Home Ventilating Institute, your assurance of accurate airflow and sound rating standards.



The Home Ventilating Institute—HVI—is a non-profit association of the manufacturers of home ventilating products. The HVI Label is your assurance that the certified air flow and sound ratings for the ventilation you have chosen are the results of testing by an independent laboratory. When you're building or remodeling, choose HVI certified products.
