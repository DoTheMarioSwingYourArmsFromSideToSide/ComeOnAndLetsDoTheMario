STYLE FOR LIFE
Hunter Introduces New Fans to the Prestige® Collection

MEMPHIS, Tenn. (January 13, 2005) – The country is experiencing an unprecedented interest in stylish, highly functional products. From coffee pots to laptops to sporty coupes, a design revolution is sweeping American life. With the home at the center of this revolution, more and more people view their living space as an extension of their personality where every room becomes a chance to express one’s own style. Hunter Prestige® fans fit right in.

Many designers and decorators regard light fixtures and hardware accessories as the “jewelry” of a home. It’s an insightful approach that makes a fine ceiling fan the ideal finishing touch in any room. The 2005 Prestige product line, found exclusively in designer showrooms, offers homeowners the broadest palette of styles, sizes and finishes ever. A series of eight collections are specifically tailored to the way we live now:
  • Europa is inspired by continental influences, from classic to contemporary.

  • American Heritage highlights uniquely American contributions to the history of design.

  • Tropic celebrates the relaxed, yet not unsophisticated casual style that has swept the fashion world.

  • Contemporary features ultra-modern designs at their most appealing.

  • Traditional proves that great design transcends time and trend.

  • Transitional crosses stylistic borders with ease, demonstrating the benefits of an eclectic approach.

  • Outdoor and Builder models prove that practical needs can be met without sacrificing beautiful appearance.

Rich woods, enduring metals, lustrous hand-applied finishes, artfully crafted
glass, and architectural details are just some of the many finely crafted elements that come together beautifully in the Hunter Prestige line of showroom fans, such as:

  • Valhalla™ - The boldness and wit of northern European style created a design revolution that continues to this day.

  • Pienza™ - The Tuscan town of Pienza has long been hailed as an architectural masterpiece that perfectly balances form and function. Qualities found in the ceiling fan that shares its name.

  • Zoë™ - A funky twist on modern minimalism. Elegant and eclectic, with a variety of lighting configurations – from undiluted Danish modern to textured linen glass.

  • Ashland™ - The refreshing simplicity of pure craftsmanship. Its quiet beauty eschews fussy details and flashy appearance for the ageless appeal of true Americana.

  • Coronado™ - A tastefully tropical design with artfully carved cherry wood blades that brings your fantasy of lush palms and island breeze to life.

  • The Piedmont™ - Relaxed yet refined. With the help of subtle details reminiscent of the American Arts and Crafts movement, it offers definite proof that one need not sacrifice comfort for style.

Throughout the Prestige collection, you’ll discover Hunter technological breakthroughs that virtually eliminate wobble, make installation a snap, and ensure whisper-quiet performance. Attention to detail is captured in the ceiling fans and in the new generation of trendsetting remotes.

Founded in 1886 and headquartered in Memphis, Tenn., Hunter Fan Co. is the world’s original ceiling fan manufacturer. Today, the company also manufactures high-performance air purifiers, low maintenance humidifiers, portable fans and energy-saving thermostats. Hunter fans and other product lines are available in retail showrooms, home centers and discount stores nationwide. For more information, consumers may call 1-800-4HUNTER or visit www.hunterfan.com.
