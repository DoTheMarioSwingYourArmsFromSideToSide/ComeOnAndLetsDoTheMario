The AirMax® Motor
The motor is the heart of any ceiling fan. Many fan manufacturers buy generic “off-the-shelf” motors to power their fans. At Hunter, we create our own motors, and design and build them specifically for ceiling fan use.

Consider our flagship motor, the ultra-powerful AirMax®. With a design so unique it’s patented, the AirMax® is built of the finest materials, including:

• A Die-Cast Aluminum Housing
• Factory-Sealed Precision Bearings
• Heavy-Gauge Copper Windings
• Silicon Steel Laminates

The AirMax® motor also includes our exclusive internal impeller. The impeller is a fan-within-a-fan that cools the motor whenever the fan is running.

Together, superior materials and superior design make the AirMax® the quietest, coolest-running, most powerful motor we’ve ever created. No wonder we back every one with a limited lifetime motor warranty.
