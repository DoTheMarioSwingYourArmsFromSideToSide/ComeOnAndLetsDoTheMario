THE STRONG SILENT TYPE
Power, Efficiency and Style All Come Together In Hunter Fan Co.’s Paramount XP™

MEMPHIS, Tenn. (June 23, 2005) — The latest addition from Hunter Fan Co. matches powerful performance with classic style in The Paramount XP.

The Paramount XP is ideal for today’s homes. Its classic styling works with a variety of decorating approaches. “This is one of the most powerful fans we’ve ever created,” said Jim Barrett, vice president of marketing and product development for Hunter Fan Co.’s ceiling fan division.

This Energy Star® rated fan has a 54-inch blade span with a 188mm x 25mm Hunter high performance motor. The Paramount XP features the EZ Hang™ hanging system and Easy Lock™ blade iron-to-motor assembly. Easy Lock allows for faster assembly and also makes cleaning the fan blades easier. 

All 13 of The Paramount XP models come equipped with dual-sided fan blades that accent a variety of housing finishes including:

• 23247 Antique Brass with five Walnut/Medium Oak blades

• 23248 Hunter Bright Brass Finish® with five Walnut/Medium Oak blades

• 23249 White with five White/Maple blades

• 23251 White/Brushed Nickel with five White/Maple blades

• 23253 Satin White with five White/Maple blades

• 23254 Brushed Nickel with five Cherry/Chestnut blades

• 23255 Antique Pewter with five Light Cherry/Walnut blades

• 23256 Weathered Bronze with five Teak/Dark Cherry blades

• 23257 Bronze Patina with five Walnut/Pickled Oak blades

• 23258 New Bronze with five Teak/Cherried Walnut blades

• 23259 Weathered Bronze with five Teak/Dark Cherry blades

• 23260 Antique Black with five Light Cherry/semi-gloss Black blades

• 23261 Provençal Gold with five Dark Walnut/Dark Cherry blades

The Paramount XP comes complete with a limited lifetime motor warranty backed by Hunter’s heritage of more than 100 years of experience producing top-of-the-industry ceiling fans.

Founded in 1886 and headquartered in Memphis, Tenn., Hunter Fan Co. is the world’s original ceiling fan manufacturer. Today, the company also manufactures high-performance air purifiers, low-maintenance humidifiers, portable fans, decorative bathroom fans, and energy-saving thermostats. Hunter fans and other product lines are available in retail showrooms and home centers nationwide. For more information, consumers may call 1-800-4HUNTER or visit www.hunterfan.com.
