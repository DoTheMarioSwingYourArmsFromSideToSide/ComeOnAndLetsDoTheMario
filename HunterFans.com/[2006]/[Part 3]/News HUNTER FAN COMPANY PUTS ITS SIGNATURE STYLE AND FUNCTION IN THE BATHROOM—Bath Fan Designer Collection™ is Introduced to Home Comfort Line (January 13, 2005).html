HUNTER FAN COMPANY PUTS ITS SIGNATURE STYLE AND FUNCTION IN THE BATHROOM—Bath Fan Designer Collection™ is Introduced to Home Comfort Line

MEMPHIS, Tenn. (January 13, 2005) – Hunter Fan Co., the leading manufacturer of ceiling fans, introduces the new Designer Collection™ line of decorative bath fans. The line has a variety of designs, including models with light, night light and accessory controls that have timer features.

“A bath fan used to be seen as more of a functional necessity than a fashionable accessory for the bathroom,” said Kevin Udy, product manager of Hunter’s bath fan division. “In today’s home designs, there is much more attention to detail in bathroom styling, yet the existing fan designs left much to be desired. Hunter’s designers have once again paired our signature whisper-quiet fan technology with home décor savvy to create a beautiful, attractive product line.”

Every bath fan in the Designer Collection is designed with a four-pole motor for high performance and has standard four-inch ducting. The bezel sizes range from the basic Spa Select™ at 9.5 square inches to the elegant Millennium™, which is slightly more than 15 inches in diameter. All models are designed for easy installation, with adjustable slider bars and are all certified by the Home Ventilating Institute. Certain models feature a light and night light. The accessory timer control allows the fan to run after leaving the bathroom, and then automatically shuts off.

The Designer Collection will be available in Lowe’s® Home Improvement Warehouse stores and lighting showrooms. The suggested retail price will range from $26.99 to $129.99, and the fans are backed by a five year limited warranty.

Founded in 1886 and headquartered in Memphis, Tenn., Hunter Fan Co. is the world’s original ceiling fan manufacturer. Today, the company also manufactures high-performance air purifiers, low-maintenance humidifiers, vaporizers, portable fans and energy-saving thermostats. Hunter fans and other product lines are available in retail showrooms, home centers, and discount stores nationwide. For more information, consumers may call 1-800-4HUNTER or visit www.hunterfan.com.
