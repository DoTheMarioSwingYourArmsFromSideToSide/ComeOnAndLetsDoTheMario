NEW OUTDOOR OPTIONS
Hunter Fan Co. Allows You to Customize Your Outdoor Ceiling Fan

MEMPHIS, Tenn. (January 13, 2005) – Individual Elements™, the latest innovation in Hunter Fan Co.’s outdoor ceiling fan line, allows the homeowner to customize the color and style of an outdoor ceiling fan to match any décor. Beautiful as well as functional, these fans make a stylish addition to any home.

Each Individual Elements fan is UL® Wet Listed to weather Mother Nature’s worst. With this new option, a homeowner can customize the Individual Elements ceiling fan to his or her own taste by choosing from two fan body finishes and adding five 52” blades. Mix-and-match options include:

Fan Blades:
Real Teak Wood
Plastic Palm in Antique Dark Finish
Synthetic Wicker in Bermuda Finish
Synthetic Wicker in Gossamer Brown Finish
 Fan Bodies:
Textured Black
Weathered Brick

“Homeowners view their living space as an extension of their personality, a way to express one’s own style,” says Jim Barrett, vice president of marketing and product development for Hunter Fan Co.’s ceiling fan division. “We want to provide options so that our fans are the perfect fit for the home, indoors or out.”

The Individual Elements ceiling fans, part of Hunter’s Prestige® line for 2005, are ideal for porches, patios, decks, gazebos, sunrooms, or even indoors. Throughout the Prestige collection, exclusively found in designer showrooms, you’ll discover Hunter technological breakthroughs that virtually eliminate wobble, make installation a snap, and ensure whisper-quiet performance. All Hunter ceiling fans are backed by more than 100 years of experience and by a Lifetime Motor Warranty.

Founded in 1886 and headquartered in Memphis, Tenn., Hunter Fan Co. is the world’s original ceiling fan manufacturer. Today, the company also manufactures high-performance air purifiers, low maintenance humidifiers, portable fans and energy-saving thermostats. Hunter fans and other product lines are available in retail showrooms, home centers and discount stores nationwide. For more information, consumers may call 1-800-4HUNTER or visit www.hunterfan.com.
