HUNTER’S BEAR CREEK OFFERS PASTORAL TASTE AND ROBUST CHARM

MEMPHIS, Tenn. (January 11, 2006) – A whimsical wilderness panorama balances the boldness of the Bear Creekä fan’s rugged wood and sturdy metalwork. Whether your rustic little cabin is in the mountains, on the lake, or in your dreams, the Bear Creek is sure to put a campfire song in your heart.

The Bear Creek (model 28523) is an unparalleled addition to Hunter’s Americana product family. Its individual charm is showcased in a rustic bark-like housing, unique weathered antler pull chains, and five gouged distressed cherry lodge blades, which are crafted from furniture grade wood veneer. With a 522 blade span mounted on a 172 mm x 20 mm motor, Bear Creek showcases the WobbleFreeä Canopy mounting system for easy installation and perfect balance. It features a hands-free loop that temporarily supports the fan during wiring, which allows for greater mounting ease. The WobbleFree Canopy’s self-aligning trilobular ball maintains full, even contact with the housing by eliminating stress points that often contribute to wobble. The Bear Creek can be mounted in flush, standard or angled fashion. 

“The Bear Creek is an exceptional example of the wide range of styles presented in our Prestigeâ Collection,” said Beau Parker, director of marketing for the ceiling fan division. “Its originality is a reflection of the focus of our Americana line.”

The Americana product family highlights uniquely American contributions to the history of design. This Prestige Collection line presents styles that range from pastoral simplicity to the art nouveau flair of an authentic replica of an early Hunter offering.

Rich woods, enduring metals, lustrous hand-applied finishes, artfully crafted glass, and architectural details are just some of the many finely crafted elements that come together beautifully in the Hunter Prestige line of showroom fans.

Founded in 1886 and headquartered in Memphis, Tenn., Hunter Fan Co. is the world’s original ceiling fan manufacturer. Today, the company also manufactures high-performance air purifiers, low maintenance humidifiers, portable fans, decorative bathroom fans and energy-saving thermostats. Hunter fans and other product lines are available in retail showrooms and home centers nationwide. For more information, consumers may call 1-800-4HUNTER or visit hunterfan.com.
