THE CASBAH PROVIDES ALLURING GLAMOUR AND ECLECTIC STYLE FOR THE HOME

MEMPHIS, Tenn. (January 11, 2006) – Exotic. Mysterious. Intriguing. The narrow winding streets, intimate cafes, and dreamlike textures of the Algerian demi-monde were stylized in a series of Hollywood film classics—and return in the languid romanticism and decorative flair of the Casbahä.

As part of the Transitional product family, the Casbah is available in two distinguished styles: antique pewter finish with faux expresso leather (model 23720) and satin bronze finish with faux acorn leather (model 23721). Both styles have four dark walnut veneer blades with a 52² blade span. The Casbah is powered by a 172 mm x 15 mm whisper-quiet AirMaxâ motor built with Hunter’s exclusive impeller (“a fan within a fan”) to keep the fan running cooler from the inside out.

For easy installation and perfect balance, the Casbah showcases the WobbleFreeä Canopy mounting system. It features hands-free loop temporarily supports the fan during wiring, which allows for greater mounting ease. The WobbleFree Canopy’s self-aligning trilobular ball maintains full, even contact with the housing by eliminating stress points that often contribute to wobble. The Casbah may be installed in flush, standard or angled mounting fashions.

“With its original cast-iron and faux leather details, the Casbah provides dash and distinction to any setting,” said Beau Parker, director of marketing for the ceiling fan division. “This model is a fine complement to our Prestigeâ Collection.”

The Transitional product family crosses stylistic borders with ease, demonstrating the benefits of an eclectic approach. The tasteful confluence of diverse styles provides this product line with unique character and widespread appeal.

Rich woods, enduring metals, lustrous hand-applied finishes, artfully crafted glass, and architectural details are just some of the many finely crafted elements that come together beautifully in the Hunter Prestige line of showroom fans.

Founded in 1886 and headquartered in Memphis, Tenn., Hunter Fan Co. is the world’s original ceiling fan manufacturer. Today, the company also manufactures high-performance air purifiers, low maintenance humidifiers, portable fans, decorative bathroom fans and energy-saving thermostats. Hunter fans and other product lines are available in retail showrooms and home centers nationwide. For more information, consumers may call 1-800-4HUNTER or visit hunterfan.com.
