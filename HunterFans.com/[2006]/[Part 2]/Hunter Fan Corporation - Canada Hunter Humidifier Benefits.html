Hunter Humidifier Benefits

1. Low Maintenance
Hunter Care Free® humidifiers are built for maximum performance with minimum routine maintenance (maintenance will vary based on individual use). Everything about our humidifiers is designed to be easy to use and easy to clean!

2. PermaWick™ Permanent Filter
The Care Free® Humidifierplus features our exclusive PermaWick™ filter, a permanent wick that’s designed to last season after season. Because it never needs to be replaced, the PermaWick saves time and money. And cleaning is simple.

3. Regulated Release™ System
Hunter’s innovative approach maintains flow and eliminates stagnant water that can breed harmful bacteria, molds and fungi.

4. Top-Down Water Dispersion (select models)
Our system spreads water evenly over the entire wick, from top to bottom, reducing performance-impeding mineral build-up.

5. Antimicrobial Properties
Key plastic parts and wicks have permanent, built-in antimicrobial properties to inhibit the growth of bacteria, molds and fungi that may affect these parts.

6. No White Dust
Our units create no white dust. So there’s nothing to settle on furnishings or to be inhaled.

7. A Wide Range of Models
There’s truly a Hunter humidifier for everyone. Models are available with Output Rates ranging from 2 gallons per day (for coverage of areas as large as 1,175 square feet) up to 5 gallons per day (for coverage of areas as large as 2,000 square feet).

8. Hunter Reliability
Every Care Free® Humidifier and Care Free® Humidifierplus is backed by a 5-year limited warranty. That’s peace of mind protection from a company that’s been in business for over a century!

*This product contains antibacterial properties, which are built-in to inhibit the growth of bacteria that may affect the plastic parts and filter (wick) in the humidifier. These properties do not extend to the water, humidified air or users. Conduct routine cleaning and maintenance based upon use and individual conditions.

† Independent study of physicians conducted 12/96
