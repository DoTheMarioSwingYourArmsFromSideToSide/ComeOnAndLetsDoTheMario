Hunter Fan Co. Introduces New Vaporizer To Home Comfort Product Line

MEMPHIS, Tenn. (January 12, 2003) – Hunter Fan Co. has introduced the Care Free® Vaporizerplus as the newest addition to the Hunter family of home comfort products. Available in one and two gallon models, these portable vaporizers can help soothe the effects of winter colds and flu.

Upper respiratory illnesses, such as colds and flu, often cause faster and deeper breathing, which dry out airway surfaces. Physicians agree that increasing the moisture in the air is a beneficial way to help relieve congestion and calm irritated and inflamed airways. The Vari-Mist™ feature on the Care Free® Vaporizerplus allows full control over the amount of vapor released into the air, so symptoms can be relieved more quickly.

The vaporizer’s uniquely designed base also makes it easier to carry and reduces the probability of being tipped over. Another important safeguard is the patented 2-in-1™ Safety Lock that keeps the tank locked until the electrical cord is detached, preventing accidental spills and access to heating elements.

Art Massey, vice president of Hunter’s Home Comfort division, said, “We’re proud that the features we’ve designed on the Care Free® Vaporizerplus provide better in-use protection, especially when used in children’s rooms.”

Hunter has made the care and maintenance of the Care Free® Vaporizerplus a cinch, with features like the ToteAway™ tank. The large tank opening provides easy access for dishwasher-safe cleaning, while the EZ Drain™ removable plug allows for complete tank drainage. And the unit’s built-in anti-microbial properties actually inhibit the growth of dangerous bacteria associated with moisture.
